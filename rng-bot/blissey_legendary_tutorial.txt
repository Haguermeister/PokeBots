Blissey's (imablisy) Legendary/Static RNG Tutorial - FireRed/LeafGreen Switch
============================================================
Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gtxa6CyTOGM

PREREQUISITES:
- Master Ball (for catching wild legendaries to check stats)
- 3-5 Rare Candies (helps identify low-level Pokemon stats)
- Options: Sound=Mono, Button Mode=Help
- Save in front of target Pokemon

EONTIMER SETUP:
- Console: GBA (even though on Switch!)
- Custom tab, 3 timers:
  - Timer 1: Milliseconds (seed timing from 10 Lines)
  - Timer 2: Advances (continue screen frames from 10 Lines)
  - Timer 3: Advances = 600 (overworld frames, NEVER CHANGE/CALIBRATE)

TEN LINES SETUP:
- Game: Switch Fire Red or Switch Leaf Green (same for all languages except Japanese)
- Sound: Mono, Button Mode: Help
- Seed Button: A, Extra Button: None
- Console: Switch 1 or Switch 2 (changes timing values)
- Target seed: blank initially, then paste target seed
- Seed ±: 50 for initial search, 20 for calibration
- Min advances: 1000, Max advances: 5000
- Required overworld frames: 600
- Method: Static 1
- Category: starters/fossils/gifts/game corner/stationary/legendaries/events/roamers
- Shininess: star/square

TIMER MATH:
- Total advances = continue_screen_frames + (overworld_frames × 2)
- Example: 425 continue + 600×2 = 1625 total advances
- Continue screen frames = total_advances - 1200 (with 600 overworld)
- Only calibrate timer 1 (seed ms) and timer 2 (continue frames)
- NEVER calibrate timer 3 (overworld frames)

ADJUSTING OVERWORLD FOR LONG ANIMATIONS:
- Default 600 works for most Pokemon
- For long animations (e.g., Deoxys): increase overworld frames to 1200+
- This reduces continue screen time but gives more overworld time
- Some targets may become impossible if animation consumes too many advances

WHEN TO PRESS A (Generation Trigger):
- NOT always the last dialogue box!
- Legendary birds: Press A IN FRONT of it (first interaction)
- Deoxys: Press A in front of the red triangle
- Ho-Oh: Press UP (no A press — walking triggers it)
- Game Corner: varies
- Full list at: pokerailrng.com

START MENU TRICK (IMPORTANT):
- After continuing/loading save, press B immediately to open start menu
- Wandering NPCs can affect RNG — start menu prevents NPC movement
- Close start menu on the 5th beep
- Talk to/interact with Pokemon on the final (6th) beep

BOOT SEQUENCE:
1. Close the game
2. Launch game → immediately press HOME
   - Switch 1: don't press too quickly or it cancels launch
   - Switch 2: can be very fast
   - OK to see Switch logo, NOT OK to see copyright/game text
3. Start EonTimer + press A to resume at the same time
4. Watch entire intro (don't press anything!)
5. When timer 1 finishes: press and HOLD A at title screen
6. Continue screen appears → wait for timer 2
7. When timer 2 finishes: press A to continue
8. Press B immediately to open start menu (NPC trick)
9. Close start menu on 5th beep of timer 3
10. Press A on final beep (generation trigger)

CALIBRATION PROCEDURE:
1. Catch the Pokemon (Master Ball recommended)
2. Check stats, nature, gender
3. Input into 10 Lines IV calculator (nature, level, stats)
4. If more than 1 result: use Rare Candy to level up, add another stat row
5. Copy "hit" seed ms value → paste into EonTimer timer 1 hit field
6. Copy "hit" continue screen frames → paste into EonTimer timer 2 hit field
7. Click EonTimer "update" button
8. DON'T adjust if within 2 advances or ~30ms — that's human/switch variance
9. Only adjust if consistently off by the same amount 5-6 times

MISSING SEED ISSUE:
- If aiming for one of the first 5-10 seeds, you might hit an unrecorded seed
- 10 Lines will show "no results" — this doesn't mean you did something wrong
- Increase seed ± range or try again
- Less likely with seeds in the middle of the list

NOTE ON 500/574 vs 600/600:
- This tutorial uses 600/600 as the standard approach
- Papa Jefe mentioned 500/574 as an alternative on YoggTwo's video
- 500/574: 1000 overworld advances (tighter, more precise for cutscene-less)
- 600/600: 1200 overworld advances (more forgiving, general purpose)
- Both are valid — 600/600 is the recommended default per Blissey
